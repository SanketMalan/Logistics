Our History

A Decade of Excellence

Founded in 2010 in a small garage in Mumbai, Logicstick has grown into India's premier logistics partner. We started with a single truck and a vision to simplify supply chains.

Today, we handle over 5 million shipments annually, connecting businesses across 19,000+ pin codes.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark for customer-centric supply chain solutions.
